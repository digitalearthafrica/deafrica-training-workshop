Session 2: Datasets
=======================

This session is all about the datasets available through Digital Earth Africa. These datasets can be visualised through the Digital Earth Africa Map, or sorted by availability in the Digital Earth Africa Metadata Explorer. We can then load specific datasets to produce colour imagery.

Click on **Digital Earth Africa products** to get started. If you would like to review Session 1 content first, click **Session 1: Introduction** in the side menu.
